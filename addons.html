Add-Ons

These are third-party projects that other people have written for use with Transmission. Many of them are cool but don't look to us for technical support...

Apps and Utilities:

  • Browser-based remote controls: Kettu,  Shift,  Tr Web Control,  flood,  combustion,  TrguiNG
  • Standalone remote controls: Transdroid, (video review), remote-gtk,  remote-gui, Kebler (Win only),  tremotesf2,  Clutch,  Electorrent,  tremc,  stig,  torque,  TrguiNG
  • Desktop remote controls: Transmission Indicator (GNOME Shell)
  • Android remote controls: tremotesf-android,  transmissionbtc,  Transmissionic,  TransmissionRemote,  Search Google Play
  • iOS remote controls: transshift,  BarMagnet
  • Telegram Remote Controls: transmission-telegram
  • RSS Tools: FlexGet
  • Browser add-ons: transmitter
  • Portable Daemons: transmission-vs,  transmission-daemon-cygwin
  • PowerShell module: Transmission (github repo)

Resources:

  • Remote control libraries: PHP-Transmission-Class, transmission_api (Ruby), Transmission: Client (Perl),  transmission-rpc (Python)
  • Erin Yueh has a writeup of porting Transmission to Android
