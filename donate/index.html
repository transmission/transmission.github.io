Thank you for thinking of donating! Please consider these two charities below that have been suggested by members of the Transmission maintainer team, or use GiveRadar to find a charity near you.

Donate to the Clean Air Task Force

The Clean Air Task Force is a global nonprofit that pushes the changes needed to reach a zero-emissions, high-energy planet at an affordable cost. It advances the most neglected, high-impact climate solutions — from carbon capture and superhot-rock geothermal to advanced nuclear and cutting methane — and is consistently ranked among the most cost-effective climate charities in the world.

Donate to the Musicians' Clinic

The New Orleans Musicians' Clinic provides affordable, comprehensive medical and mental-health care to the musicians and culture-bearers who keep New Orleans' living traditions alive. Since 1998 it has kept thousands of working artists healthy and performing, using music-as-medicine to improve the health of the whole community.
